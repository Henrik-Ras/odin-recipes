Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made with layers of flat pasta sheets, savory meat sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and melted cheese.It's a hearty and flavorful baked casserole that's often enjoyed as a comforting meal, perfect for gatherings or cozy dinners at home.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Spinach (optional)
  • Seasonings (salt, pepper, basil, oregano, garlic powder)

  1. Gather lasagna noodles, ground beef or Italian sausage, tomato sauce, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, spinach (optional), and seasonings.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Brown meat in a skillet, add tomato sauce, and season to taste.
  3. Combine ricotta cheese with beaten eggs and season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  4. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and shredded cheeses. Repeat until ingredients are used, ending with a layer of cheese on top.
  5. Cover with foil and bake in a preheated oven at specified temperature for 30-40 minutes.
  6. Remove foil during the last 10 minutes to brown cheese.
  7. Let lasagna rest for a few minutes before slicing. Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired.
